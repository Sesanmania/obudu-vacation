The Main Causes of Stress in College Students

Many people look back and consider their college years to be some of the best years of their lives. But if you ask a student who is currently enrolled, they are likely to describe the experience as stressful. With pressure to perform, to get good grades, to choose a career path in life it is no wonder that college students often report that their stress levels are high. But there are a few ways to manage stress in your late teens and early twenties as you explore the college experience.
